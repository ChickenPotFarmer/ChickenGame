Cartoon pig for Unity
Perfect for endless runners, 2D or 3D,
Mobile Friendly!

There are two prefabs with two models:

Piggy -> 884 triangles

Piggy_3k -> 3720 triangles

Both with 7 animations:

- Idle, frames: 5-170
- Run, frames: 175-193
- StrafeL, frames: 195-213
- StrafeR, frames: 215-233
- Attack, frames: 235-253
- Jump, frames: 255-273
- GetHit, frames: 275-325

Material and color texture provided,
well unfolded UVs to create your own skins.

There is a Scene in Scenes folder to review all the animations and the two prefabs.
